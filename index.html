Orient Phase

What you'll do:

  • Explore 3-5 different academic disciplines
  • Attend introductory sessions with subject experts
  • Complete short exploratory projects in each field
  • Identify your strongest interests and aptitudes

Key Benefits:

  • Discover your true academic passions
  • Make informed decisions about your future studies
  • Gain exposure to university-level subjects
  • Build foundational knowledge across disciplines

Duration:

15 hours

Prepare Phase

What you'll do:

  • Work 1-on-1 with a PhD mentor in your chosen field
  • Develop a specialized research project
  • Attend advanced subject workshops
  • Build a portfolio of academic work

Key Benefits:

  • Deep dive into your chosen subject area
  • Gain university-level research experience
  • Develop critical thinking and analytical skills
  • Create work that strengthens your applications

Duration:

25 hours

Apply Phase

What you'll do:

  • Receive personalized application strategy
  • Perfect your personal statement and essays
  • Prepare for admissions tests and interviews
  • Learn scholarship application techniques

Key Benefits:

  • Maximize your chances of admission
  • Present your best self to admissions committees
  • Reduce stress through expert guidance
  • Access insider knowledge about top universities

Duration:

10 hours
